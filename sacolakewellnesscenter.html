GOAL: Expand considerations of materiality and architectural atmosphere to include water, in its various states in the context of a bathhouse. Consider the therapeutic benefits and cultural significance of public baths and spas, and develop an architectural proposal for a Wellness Center. Reference ancient traditions that utilize water of various temperatures and states to rejuvenate and heal.

SITE: Near the Appalachian Mountain Club (AMC) Highland Center Lodge in the mountatins of New Hampshire.

PROGRAM:

  • Cold Pool
  • Warm Pool
  • Hot Pool
  • Dry Sauna
  • Herbal Steam Room
  • Relaxation Space
  • Securable Entrance and Mudroom
  • Changing Facilities and Service Areas

PROJECT PROCESS:

  • Three Basins
  • Thermae
  • Saco Lake Wellness Center
