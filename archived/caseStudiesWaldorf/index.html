Case Studies - Waldorf School of Pittsburgh

Carnegie Mellon University

Second Year | Spring 2017

Goal:

This case study project investigated Waldorf School of Pittsburgh, a local private school in Pittsburgh, PA. Diagrams and drawings were created to illustrate and investigate their curriculum approach, facility layout, urban location, and ideas to improve their school.

In collaboration with Ghalya Al Sanea, Gunn Chaiyapatranun, Jack Fogel, Alina Kramkova, Judy Li, Michael Powell, and Hamza Qureshi
