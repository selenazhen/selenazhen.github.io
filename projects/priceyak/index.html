PriceYak

Ecommerce Website | 2017

Identity | Logo | Design | Frontend

Goal:

When working for Zinc Technologies in Summer 2017, I redeveloped their main product, PriceYak with a full webpage remodel, including branding, logo work, and front end.

From the website: PriceYak is the most advanced automation suite for retail dropship arbitrage. PriceYak supports selling on eBay, Amazon, or Shopify and sourcing from many retailers including Amazon (.com, .ca and .co.uk), Walmart, and Costco. PriceYak's tools automate the operation of your store so you can free you and your staff's time for the most important thing: growing your business.
